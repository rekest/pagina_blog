How to assemble a PC?

So, if you would like to buy a PC for parts, or you want an introduction to PC hardware, you should know how to assemble a PC.

In this simple tutorial, we will see, how to assemble a DESKTOP PC.

STEP 1

Buy your components: make sure they are compatible with each other, they do not have a bottleneck and that they fit into your tower.
You have to buy:
  • Coolers
  • Motherboard
  • Processor
  • Graphic card
  • A tower
  • Cables
  • RAM memory
  • A WIFI card (optional)
  • A power supply
Make sure, that the RAM memory fit properly in each socket (DDR1, DDR2, DDR3, DDR4), also with the processor and it socket (the socket change depending of the motherboard), not all
the processors fit properly in any motherboard. Exist motherboards for Intel processor´s and AMD processor´s!.

STEP 2

Order your space: find a space to assemble the pc. A table is an ideal place. Make sure that the table has a nice space to move things.

STEP 3

Unboxing: Unbox your components carefully, ¡They are fragile! It is recommended to use gloves, an antistatic wristband, or just clothes that don't generate a lot of static.

STEP 4

Start assembling: puts the power supply in its place (this depends on the tower).
After that, take your motherboard and put the different components in their places. Make sure the components fit properly in each socket, and Make sure that the cover that covers the entrances of the motherboard is on.

STEP 5

Connecting: Take your cables and let´s go with the connection.
